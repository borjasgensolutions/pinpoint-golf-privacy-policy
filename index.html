PinPoint Golf

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how PinPoint Golf collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, the app may collect or process:

  • Location data if you choose to use your current location to estimate distance and driving time to golf courses.
  • Purchase and subscription information needed to verify and restore paid access purchased through Apple.
  • Push notification data such as your device token, notification settings, and a backend device identifier if you enable notifications.
  • App usage inputs such as the addresses or locations you enter to search, map, or filter golf information.

How We Use Information

  • To show golf courses, events, and related map information.
  • To calculate approximate distance and drive-time information.
  • To provide optional push notifications based on your selected preferences.
  • To verify subscription status and enable paid features.
  • To maintain app security, reliability, and performance.

Location Information

The app only uses your device location if you grant permission. Location is used to help calculate distance and travel-related information inside the app.

You can deny or revoke location access at any time in your device settings.

Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, the app may send your device token and related notification preference settings to our backend services so notifications can be delivered according to your preferences.

You can turn notifications off at any time in the app or in your device settings.

Purchases and Subscriptions

Purchases are processed by Apple. We may receive and store limited purchase or entitlement details needed to confirm your subscription status, restore access, and prevent duplicate entitlement issues.

We do not receive your full payment card details.

Third-Party Services and Websites

The app may load content from third-party services or open third-party golf-related websites, such as map providers, event registration pages, and course websites. Those third parties may collect information under their own privacy policies.

Tracking and Advertising

PinPoint Golf is not designed for cross-app tracking and does not use your data for third-party targeted advertising.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to operate the app, maintain notification preferences, verify subscriptions, resolve support issues, and comply with legal obligations.

Your Choices

  • You can decline location access.
  • You can disable push notifications.
  • You can manage subscriptions through your Apple account settings.
  • You can stop using the app at any time.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
